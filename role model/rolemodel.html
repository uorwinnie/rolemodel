What is a feminist?

A feminist is a person who supports or engages in feminism the advocacy of women's rights on the basis of the equality of the sexes. It's about respecting diverse women's experiences, identities, knowledge and strengths, and striving to empower all women to realize their full rights. And my role model is one of the feminists.

Personal Information Emma's full name is Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson and she was born on 15 April 1990, which makes her a 33 years old, in Paris. Both of her parents were lawyers. Her parents divorced when she was young, and Watson moved to England to live with her mother in Oxfordshire while spending weekends at her father's house in London. She has a younger brother, Alexander Chris Watson who is a British actor and model. She has two stepbrothers from her mother's remarriage and three half siblings from her father's second marriage.
The release of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone in 2001 was her debut screen performance. The film broke records for opening-day sales and opening-weekend takings and was the highest-grossing film of 2001.

Emma Watson said that she was a feminist herself and she became a U.N. Goodwill Ambassador in 2014 and she has championed the U.N. Women's HeForShe campaign.HeForShe is an invitation for men and people of all genders to stand in solidarity with women to create a bold, visible and united force for gender equality. The men of HeForShe aren't on the sidelines. They're working with women and with each other to build businesses, raise families, and give back to their communities.
Using this campaign, she encouraged everyone, whether male or female, to participate in change and action against negative labeling as stakeholders to achieve gender equality. Her speech for HeForShe campaign in UN in 2014 was quite famous among people who are interested in gender equality.
